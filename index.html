About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Financial & Actuarial Mathematics at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU). Currently, I also serve as the Program Director for the MSc Actuarial Science program.

My research sits at the intersection of actuarial science, stochastic control, and mathematical modeling in insurance risk. I primarily focus on optimal dividend strategies, capital injection mechanisms, mortality forecasting, and applying modern machine learning methodologies to insurance risk evaluation.
