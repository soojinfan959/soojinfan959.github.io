About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Financial & Actuarial Mathematics at Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU), where I also serve as the Program Director for the MSc Actuarial Science program. Prior to joining XJTLU, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics & Statistics at Concordia University, Montreal, Canada.

Research Interests

My research focuses on mathematical and stochastic modeling within insurance risk and actuarial science. Specifically, my areas of interest include:

  • Stochastic control problems in insurance and finance
  • Stochastic processes
  • Reinforcement learning in insurance and finance
  • Machine learning in actuarial science
